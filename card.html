Polar Bear

In Inuit, the polar bear name (Nanuq) means "an animal worthy of great respect."

  • Scientic Name: Ursus maritimus
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: Artic circle
  • Diet: Ice seals

Due to climate change, the polar bear is classified as a vulnerable speicies. The change in the weather is causing ice to melt and results in habitat loss for the bears.
